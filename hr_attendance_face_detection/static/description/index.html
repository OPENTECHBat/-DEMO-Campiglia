Face Recognition Attendance with GPS Tracking and Multi-Geofencing Control

Daily attendance tracking is a critical component of effective workforce management. However, traditional methods such as manual registers, access cards, or PIN-based systems are often prone to errors, manipulation, and inefficiencies. The TechUltra Face Recognition Attendance System with GPS Tracking and Multi-Geofencing Control transforms attendance management with AI-powered, contactless facial recognition seamlessly integrated into the Odoo HR suite.
Employees can mark attendance simply by facing the camera no physical contact, cards, or manual input required. Advanced liveness detection ensures that only real, authorized users can check in or check out, effectively eliminating proxy attendance and fraudulent practices.

To further strengthen security and operational flexibility, the system supports Multiple Geofence Locations. Organizations can define and manage multiple attendance zones, such as different office branches, warehouses, project sites, or remote work locations. Each geofence is configured with its own latitude, longitude, and allowable radius (in meters).
Employees must be physically present within any authorized geofence location to successfully mark attendance using face recognition. If an employee attempts to check in or check out from outside all configured geofenced areas, the system automatically restricts attendance marking.
With real-time GPS validation, intelligent multi-location geofencing, instant attendance logging, and seamless integration, HR teams gain enhanced accuracy, reduced administrative overhead, and stronger control over attendance policies ultimately driving higher operational efficiency and workforce productivity.

What is the Face Recognition Attendance with GPS Tracking and Multi-Geofencing Control?

The Face Recognition Attendance System is a contactless, AI-driven attendance solution that identifies employees using advanced facial recognition technology. It integrates seamlessly with the Odoo HR module to enable real-time attendance tracking. The system also supports GPS based multi geofencing locations, ensuring that employees can check in or check out only when they are physically present within the configured companies locations and defined radius.

How accurate is the facial recognition technology?

Our system uses AI-powered facial recognition algorithms with over 99% accuracy, ensuring reliable attendance logging even in varying lighting conditions and environments.

Is physical contact or biometric touch required?

No. The system is completely contactless, promoting hygiene and safety. Employees simply look into the camera to mark their attendance.

How fast is the attendance process?

Check-in and check-out are instantaneous, with real-time feedback provided to the employee. The system processes and logs data within seconds.

Can it be integrated with our existing Odoo HR modules?

Absolutely. The system is built for seamless integration with existing Odoo HR modules, including Employee Management, Leave Tracking, and Attendance Reporting.

What is multi geofencing and how does it work in this system?

Multi Geofencing allows organizations to define a companies locations using latitude and longitude along with a configurable radius in meters. Employees must be physically present within this defined area to successfully mark attendance. If the employee is outside the permitted radius, the system automatically blocks check-in or check-out.

Does the system support GPS location tracking?

Yes, the system automatically captures GPS coordinates during attendance marking, validates the employee's distance from the company location, and resolves the address for accurate location verification.

Is real-time attendance feedback available?

Yes, the system provides instant feedback during check-in and check-out, allowing employees to know immediately whether attendance has been successfully recorded or restricted due to location validation.

Can employees use this system for both check-in and check-out?

Yes. The system supports both check-in and check-out using face recognition with geofence validation.

What happens if an employee tries to mark attendance outside a geofence?

The system automatically blocks the check-in or check-out attempt and notifies the employee that they are outside the authorized attendance area.
